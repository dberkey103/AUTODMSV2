Side states: `Dead / Lost` (deal fell through), `Unwound` (deal reversed after the fact).

## Data Model Implications (Backend / Supabase Tables)

**Inventory side:**
- `vehicles` — VIN, stock #, year/make/model/trim, mileage, status, lifecycle dates
- `vehicle_costs` — cost type, amount, vendor, date (many per vehicle)
- `vehicle_photos` — URL, sequence, uploaded_by, uploaded_at
- `acquisitions` — source type/details, buyer rep, date
- `transports` — hauler, pickup, ETA, cost, status
- `inspections` — inspector, date, visual + mechanical notes
- `recon_items` — assigned to, status, parts cost, labor cost
- `inventory_feeds` — export targets + last-export status per vehicle

**Customer / sales side:**
- `customers` — name, contact, DL, DOB, address, co-buyer link
- `leads` — source, vehicle_id, customer info, assigned salesperson, status, conversation log
- `lead_messages` — every AI/auto-responder/manual message in thread
- `appointments` — date, time, salesperson, customer, status
- `deals` — customer, vehicle, salesperson, manager, type, status, all numbers
- `trade_ins` — VIN, allowance, payoff, lender, attached to deal
- `credit_apps` — applicant + co-applicant, employment, income, encrypted SSN
- `lender_submissions` — lender, status, terms, stips
- `deal_documents` — generated docs, signature status, signed PDF URLs
- `funding_events` — funding date, amount, reserve

**Accounting / reporting:**
- `commissions` — per deal, per salesperson
- `accounting_exports` — log of reports run, period covered

**System:**
- `users` — with roles (RBAC already in progress)
- `activity_log` — audit trail
- `alerts` — outbound email/SMS log
